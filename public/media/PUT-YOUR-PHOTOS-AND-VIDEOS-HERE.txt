Drop your photos (.jpg/.png/.webp) and videos (.mp4) into THIS folder.

Then reference them in src/main.jsx with a leading slash, e.g.:

  url:   "/media/bali-coconut.jpg"
  video: "/media/goa-clip.mp4"

That's it — no uploading or accounts needed. See SETUP.md for online hosting
options (Cloudinary etc.) if you'd rather not store big files here.
